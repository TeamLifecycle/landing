How do you define a user?
A 'User' is an end-user of your web or mobile app that you track inside of the Lifecycle CRM. This is typically a registered user of your product that logs in.
How is pricing calculated when my 'User' count changes?
Pricing for the Campaigns & Conversations packages are based on the total number of 'Users' you track in the Lifecycle CRM. Your price can increase or decrease based on the total number of tracked 'Users' increasing or decreasing.
